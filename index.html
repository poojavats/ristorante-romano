Our Story

Ciao, Hello!

At Ristorante Romano, we are dedicated to excellent food, friendly service, a warm atmosphere, and value for money. Our menu is designed to meet all tastes and includes meats, seafood, pasta, pizza, vegetarian, gluten-free, and vegan dishes.

We are very proud to source the freshest local produce here in Cork and Carrigaline to bring you the best quality food available. All our food is freshly prepared every day in our kitchen, including all our sauces, pastas, breads, and ice creams.
